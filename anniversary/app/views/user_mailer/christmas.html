New Trees This Season

Four Great Choices


This Christmas season, Chuck is unveiling four new trees, and the first folks to get a crack at these festive firs are Chuck Talk subscribers like you!

We open for business on Dec. 1 for newsletter readers and Dec. 7 for the general public, so stop by soon to take a gander at the new additions. They're limited, and we have a feeling they'll disappear fast!

Featured Trees

Balsam Fir

This classic tree style features shorter, flatter needles that will stand up to the wear and tear of a household with children and/or pets. Use of these trees date as far back as the 1600's, and are a lovely dark green color that goes well with the season.

Deodar Cedar

Known for their use in helping make coffins for mummies in ancient Egypt, these historical trees are most often seen in the HImalayas and have blue-green needles. This is Chuck's favorite new tree, and a unique addition to any living room.

Scotch Pine

Not just a punchilne from a famous Monty Python sketch, the mighty Scotch Pine is one of the nation's best-selling trees and a staple of countless homes across this great country. The Scotch has a lovely aroma and a classic storybook look.
