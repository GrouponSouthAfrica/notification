Egg Hunt & Picnic

Easter is almost upon us! Fellowship Church is proud to announce the return of our annual Egghunt and Picnic. This is a wonderful opportunity for the whole family to spend time with old and new friends and neighbors.

This year our Egg Hunt is divided by age group: infants and toddlers, elementary school-age, and kids 12 and up. Following the Hunt, we invite you to break bread on the North lawn. Please bring your own food and drinks, and bring a little extra for sharing. Don’t forget a blanket!
